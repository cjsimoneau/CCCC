Game Rules

How to Play:

  • Each square costs $5
  • Click squares to select them (you can select multiple!)
  • Enter your name and click "Claim Selected" to claim all at once
  • After all 100 squares are filled, numbers will be randomly assigned

How to Win:

  • Winners determined at end of each quarter (1st, 2nd, 3rd, Final)
  • Look at the last digit of each team's score
  • Find where those numbers intersect - that's the winning square!
  • Each quarter winner receives $62.50

Payout Structure (50/50):

  • Total pot: $500
  • Winners receive: $250 total ($62.50 per quarter)
  • Charity receives: $250 total
