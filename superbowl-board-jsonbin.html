Game Rules

How to Play:

  • Each square costs $5
  • Select a square and enter your name to claim it
  • After all 100 squares are filled, the numbers 0-9 will be randomly assigned to each team

How to Win:

  • Winners are determined at the end of each quarter (1st, 2nd, 3rd, and Final)
  • Look at the last digit of each team's score
  • Find where those two numbers intersect on the board - that's the winning square!
  • Each quarter winner receives $62.50 (50/50 split)

Payout Structure (50/50):

  • Total pot: $500 (100 squares √ó $5)
  • Winners receive: $250 total ($62.50 per quarter)
  • Charity receives: $250 total

Example: If at the end of the 1st quarter the score is Team 1: 14, Team 2: 7, the winning square is where "4" (Team 1) and "7" (Team 2) intersect. Winner gets $62.50!
